== Description ==

Start earning revenues from the least expected page in your blog, the Forgot Your Password page, with this uber cool affiliate plugin. This plugin adds a short recommendation link to users to download SafeWallet Password Manager at the bottom of the Forgot Your Password page of your blog with your unique affiliate ID. Earn 40% of each SafeWallet license sold from your affiliated site - generate revenues from a page that had no commercial value up until this plugin.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `wp-safewallet` directory to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress admin control panel
3. Go to "SafeWallet Affiliate" section in the "Settings" section of your WP admin control panel, fill in your email address in the Initial Setup section and press the "Request affiliate ID" button.

That's it!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do you track users arriving from my site

Each user arriving to our site from your unique affiliate link is marked with a cookie including your affiliate ID. This cookie will mark each purchase this user is doing inside our site for 1 full year, so you will be receiving affiliate payments for any sale for these users for a full year (might be multiple sales).

= How much will I get for sales arriving from my site

40% of each sale price. No other fees.

= How do you pay

We pay via PayPal. We pay at the start of each month. Payments for each affiliate are accumulated till we reach a $100 USD threshold. If you would like to get paid in other methods please contact us via affiliates@sbsh.net.

= How do you know my PayPal/payment details

After you setup your affiliate account through the system our team will contact you via email when the time arrives for the first payment to obtain the full details. Following payments will be directed to the same information you provide for the first payment. To update your payment details please contact us at affiliates@sbsh.net.
